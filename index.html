About

üéì Chaudhary Charan Singh University (CCSU), Meerut Chaudhary Charan Singh University, commonly known as CCSU, is a well-known public state university located in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 1965 to meet the higher-education needs of students in Western Uttar Pradesh.üè´ History and Background The university was originally named Meerut University. Later, it was renamed Chaudhary Charan Singh University in honor of Chaudhary Charan Singh, the former Prime Minister of India and a strong supporter of farmers and education. Over the years, CCSU has grown into one of the largest universities in Uttar Pradesh. üåü Accreditation and Recognition CCSU is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC). It has received a NAAC A++ grade, which reflects high academic standards and quality education. The university is also listed in national and international ranking systems. üå≥ Campus and Facilities The university campus covers around 222 acres and provides a clean and green environment. Major facilities include: Central Library Modern laboratories Hostels for boys and girls Sports complex and gymnasium Medical center Auditorium Canteen, bank, and post office Computer centers and research facilities
